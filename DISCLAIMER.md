# Usage Disclaimer

Last reviewed: July 15, 2026

Daily Market Tracker retrieves and summarizes publicly available Yahoo Finance
daily-bar data for six market indicators. It records opening-gap observations,
generates a Market Fingerprint visualization, and stores derived values in a
historical CSV for long-term market observation and research.

The project is intended for low-volume automated use: at most one scheduled
collection per U.S. trading day, plus occasional manual backfills. It is not
designed for high-frequency trading, investment execution, bulk scraping,
bulk redistribution of market data, or use as a production market-data feed.

This repository does not host or redistribute proprietary market datasets,
paid financial content, provider logos, or third-party trademarked assets.
Generated files may contain ticker symbols, prices, dates, and simple derived
values obtained from Yahoo Finance at the time of collection.

The hosted Streamlit dashboard uses Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity
to process usage and technical analytics data under their respective terms and
privacy policies.

Users are responsible for complying with current Yahoo Finance terms of use,
exchange data rules, and applicable laws before using, publishing, or
redistributing data generated by this project. Nothing in this repository
grants permission to copy, resell, redistribute, or commercially exploit
Yahoo Finance or exchange-provided market data.

## No Investment Advice

This project is for informational observation and personal record-keeping
only. It does not provide investment, financial, tax, legal, or trading
advice, and it does not predict market direction.

The Market Fingerprint, opening gaps, and any future analysis derived from
them are descriptive calculations, not recommendations to buy, sell, hold, or
trade any instrument. Users remain responsible for their own decisions and
should consult qualified professionals where appropriate.

## Data Limitations

Yahoo Finance data can be delayed, revised, unavailable, or defined according
to provider-specific session conventions. Daily Market Tracker uses Yahoo
Finance daily close and daily open fields consistently for all instruments.
The initial `2026-07-15` history row is a manually collected seed from the
prototype; later rows are collected automatically.

Historical values may differ from later downloads if Yahoo Finance revises
previously published daily data. Daily Market Tracker records observations at
the time of collection and does not automatically rewrite historical records
unless explicitly backfilled by the user.
